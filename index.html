About me

I am a B.I Analyst and Data Scientist with a solid background in software engineering from Yaounde , Cameroon. I build systems that help managers make informed decisions by transforming raw data into actionable insights.

To achieve this, I extract data from various sources, whether through APIs, web scraping, or native programming, then transform, clean, and load it into a data warehouse. Once the data is prepared, I define key performance indicators (KPIs) and perform exploratory, descriptive, and inferential analyses as part of the Business Intelligence(B.I) process. Moreover, I apply machine learning and big data algorithms to uncover patterns in the data and generate accurate predictions. Finally, I encapsulate and deploy the AI models I develop into user-friendly applications, which can be used either within a company’s own systems or through the cloud, ensuring that predictions and insights are easily accessible for decision-making.
